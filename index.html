About Me

I exited undergraduate (at the beautiful and excellent College of William and Mary) with a physics degree and a passion for interdisciplinary studies. I love modeling emergent, and self-organizing, phenomena in nature. Statistical mechanics studies as a whole are all fascinating.


Physics wired my brain for problem solving and I've been recently enjoying applying that to the IT field. Coding and web development are endlessly deep subjects with incredible power and flexibility in their applications, exactly like my physics degree.


Currently, I am attending a coding bootcamp at University of Richmond (it rocks) and working for Starbucks as a shift supervisor. Free coffee is a mighty useful perk for those late coding nights.
